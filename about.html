About AudioFlux

A modern platform built to connect artists, tools, and music services in one place.

Our Mission

AudioFlux was created to help musicians and creators discover the best music platforms, distribution tools, and analytics services available today. Our goal is to simplify the music journey by bringing everything into one clean, accessible experience.

What We Focus On

Key values that guide the AudioFlux experience

Clarity

Clear information about platforms, features, and tools without unnecessary complexity.

Accessibility

Designed for beginners and creators who want fast, intuitive access to music services.

Growth

Helping artists grow through better exposure, distribution, and monetization options.

Who We Are

AudioFlux is a student-built web project focused on modern music platforms. The site was designed as a clean, visually rich showcase that demonstrates front-end fundamentals such as layout, responsiveness, and UI consistency.

Why AudioFlux

Music creators often need to jump between multiple platforms to distribute, analyze, and monetize their work. AudioFlux brings clarity by presenting the most popular services in a single, structured experience.

What We Learn

This project emphasizes semantic HTML, modern CSS techniques, and design systems without relying on JavaScript. It reflects a strong foundation in web development best practices.

Our Journey

Idea & Planning

The AudioFlux concept started as a learning project focused on building a modern, visually consistent website using pure HTML and CSS.

Design & Layout

A glassmorphism-inspired design system was created, emphasizing gradients, spacing, typography, and reusable UI components.

Content & Structure

Pages were structured semantically with sections, cards, and services to clearly present music platforms and project goals.
